About Me

My name is Connor. I like to make things. I also like to snowboard, and longboard, and rock climb. I also like to bake bread and I do some blacksmithing. I go to McGill University where I study mechanical engineering. I made this website to document the projects that I do in my spare time. I get so much help from people online who are good about posting their projects, and I hope I can help some other people in the same way.
